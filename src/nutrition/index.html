Practical questions CCC helps answer

These are the kinds of nutrition questions users can explore over time by logging consistently.

Does hydration affect inflammation?

CCC helps users compare hydration habits against inflammation and recovery patterns across multiple days.

Does coffee count toward hydration?

Moderate coffee intake can still contribute to total fluid intake, but CCC encourages users to compare caffeine habits, water intake, energy, and recovery patterns to see what works best personally.

Do simpler meals improve digestion?

Keeping meals more consistent can make it easier to identify whether digestion improves alongside better readiness.

What meals show up on my best days?

Over time, CCC can help users connect stronger readiness days to repeatable meal and recovery patterns.
