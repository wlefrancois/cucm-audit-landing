How it works

1) Log → 2) Learn → 3) Execute

You log the same handful of signals daily. CCC highlights what correlates to better sleep, energy, body composition, and training output.

  • Log: diet adherence, caffeine, electrolytes, sleep, training
  • Learn: trendline + weekly pattern summary
  • Execute: next-week focus + small adjustments
